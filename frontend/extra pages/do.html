      DOs & Don'ts






    *Here are some guidelines which you should follow and avoid while visiting Uttar Pradesh




    DOs:

    • 1. Respect the traditions and culture of Uttar Pradesh and its people.

    • 2. Wear descent dresses while visiting religious places.

    • 3. Spend your money wisely.

    • 4. Exchange oney only at official exchange counters and banks.

    • 5. Only hires guides that are approved by Government and have license.

    • 6. Learn few words of local language that are commonly used here.

    • 7. Buy items that depict the true essence of Uttar Pradesh and are not available elsewhere.




    Don'ts:

    • 1. Do not litter, help us in maintaining our cities clean and green.

    • 2. Do not take any photographs that may make people embarrassed.

    • 3. Don't smoke, don't drink alcohol or not use drugs at public places and religious spots.

    • 4. Don't touch or harass animal and support conservation of animal by paying entrances fees to park and conserved sites.

    • 5. Don't buy crafts or products made from skin of endangered animals.

    • 6. Don't not drive on Indian roads unless you are well-aware about Indian roads and drive to the left of the road.

    • 7. Don't give any offencive comment about any religion; it could be controversial.

    • 8. Don't buy or book hotels/bus/air/railways tickets through strangers or unauthorized agents.

    • 9. Don't hire transportation from unlicensed operators.
