      FAQs






    AGRA



    Q. What are the visiting hours of Taj Mahal and When does it remain closed?

    Visiting hours are sunrise to sunset. Taj Mahal remains closed on every Friday.



    Q. What are the charges of night viewing of the Taj Mahal ?

    Rate of night Viewing Ticket:


    India(Adult): Rs 510/-


    Foreigner(Adult): Rs 750/-


    Child(3-15 years): Rs 500/-




    Q. Is there any timing for night viewing of the Taj Mahal?

    Yes, night viewed is allowed from 08:30 PM to 12:30 AM only and the duration of visit is 30 minutes.



    Q. Where can I get the Tickets for night viewing of Taj Mahal?

    The night viewing tickets of the Taj Mahal can be booked from the Booking Counter located in the office of Archaeological Survey of India, Agra Circle, 22 The Mall,Agra, Uttar Pradesh(Ph:0562-2227263) from 10 AM to 6 PM.For night viewing of Taj Mahal tickets can be booked in One day advance. The night viewing tickets can be cancelled on the same booking counter on same date of viewing up to 1 PM with cancellation charge of 25% of the ticket.



    Q. What is the limit of batch allowed to view the Taj Mahal?

    The maximum number of people allowed is 400, divided in 8 batches of 50 people each.






    LUCKNOW



    Q. What is the time of visiting Bada-Imambara and Chota-Imambara?

    The time to visit Bada-Imambara is 6:00AM to 5:00PM.



    Q. What are the charges of visiting Bada-Imambara and Chota-Imambara?

    Indians: 50/-
    Foreigners: 500/-



    Q. What is the best time to visit to visit Rumi Darwaza and what is the entry fees?

    Visitng Rumi Darwaza in the early morning or late afternoon can provide a better experience as the temperature are usually more comfortable and there is no entry fee or charges.



    Q. What are the visiting hours of Lucknow Zoo and When does it remains close?

    Visiting hours are 8:00AM to 6:00PM. It remains closed on every "Monday".



    Q. What are the charges of viewing The Lucknow Zoo?

    Children above 12 years: 80/-
    Kids (5 to 12): 40/-
    Teachers: 80/-
    Video Camera: 100/-






    MATHURA-VRINDAVAN



    Q. What is timing of Darshan in Vrindavan and Mathura?

    Morning Darshan: Usually from around 8:00AM to 12:00PM.
    Evening Darshan: Typically from around 4:00PM to 8:00PM.



    Q. What are the visiting hours for the Nidhivan Temple, and it is accessible at night?

    Visiting hours is same as other temples in Mathura-Vrindavan. Nidhivan is closed at night, based on a local belief that Lord Krishna still performs "raas leela" there.






    GORAKHPUR



    Q. What is the best time to visit Gorakhnath temple and what are the charges?

    The temple is open for visitors everyday from 4:00AM to 10:00PM and it is free of cost. For an extra fee you can enjoy a boat ride in the Pond located within the temple complex and it charge 20 rupees per person.



    Q. What is the time to visit Nauka Vihar and it's light & fountain show ?

    Morning to Evening: 5:00AM to 8:15PM daily.
    Fountain & Light show: 6:00PM to 9:00PM.



    Q. What are the visiting hours of Gorakhpur Zoo and When does it remains closed ?

    Time to visit is 9:00AM to 5:00PM and it remains closed on Monday.



    Q. What are the charges for viewing the Zoo?

    Indian Citizens: 50/-.
    Foreigner Citizens: 400-.






    AYODHYA



    Q. What is the timing for Darshan in Shri Ram Janamabhoomi?

    Devotees can enter in Ram Mandir for Darshan between 6:30AM and 9:30PM.



    Q. Is there any entry passes available for Darshan aand Aarti and what is the cost for entry passes?

    Yes, Entry passes for Darshan and aarti are freee but, must be booked online in advance through the official Shri Ram Janmabhoomi Teerth Kshetra Trust Website. You'll need to provide your name, age, Aadhar number, Mobile number, and city to book.



    Q. What is the timing for attending aarti of Ram Lala?

    Major aarti are held at 4AM (Mangala Aarti), 6:15AM (Shringar Aarti) and 10PM (Shayan Aarti), requiring a free pass.



    Q. What is the duration for Darshan?

    A Darshan can be completed within 60 to 75 minutes.






    PRAYAGRAJ



    Q. When to visit Praygraj for Snan?

    Visit praygraj for Snan on:
    Mauni Amavasya
    Basant Panchami
    Achla Saptami
    Maghi Purnima




    Q. When did "Magh Mela" and "Kumbh Mela" occur?

    "Magh Mela" occurs every year in between January and February and "Kumbh Mela" occurs every 12th year.



    Q. What is the timing and entry fee for viewing Allahabad Fort?

    Time to visit Allahabad Fort is 7:00AM to 6:00PM.
    Entry fee:
    For Indians:20/-
    For Foreigners:250/-






    VARANASI



    Q. What is the timing for Darshan at Kashi Vishwanath Temple and is there any entry pass available for Darshan?

    It open from 4:00AM to 11:00AM, reopening at 1:00PM and closing again at 10:00PM. No, there is no entry passes available.



    Q. What is the timing for Darshan at Annpurna Temple?

    It opens from 5:30AM to 11:00AM, and from 1:00PM to 9:00PM.



    Q. What is the timing for Darshan at Tulsi Manas Mandir?

    It opens from 5:30AM to 12:00AM, and from 3:30PM to 9:00PM.



    Q. Is there any charges for visiting Ghats?

    No, There is no charges for visiting Ghats.



    Q. Is there any Dress Code for Sparsh Darshan at Kashi Vishwanath Temple?

    Yes, if you want sparsh darshan then men are expected to wear Dhoti-Kurta and women a saree.
